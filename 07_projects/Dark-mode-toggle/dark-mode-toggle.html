Dark / Light Mode Toggle

A tiny accessible example that remembers your preference and respects system settings.

Preview

This area shows how the page colors update when you switch themes.

Notes

  • Uses data-theme="dark" on <html> to switch themes.
  • Preference is saved to localStorage under theme.
  • Respects prefers-color-scheme on first load if no saved preference exists.
Tip: press the D key to toggle theme (keyboard shortcut).
